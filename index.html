about us

Chef Antonio Garuzo

why choose us?

The Bake in Care Bakery was founded in August 1983 by Antonio Garuzo, an Italian baker, who emigrated to Columbus, Ohio. For nearly 40 years, the Bake in Care Shop has used the finest ingredients to produce cakes, pastries, cookies, and tortes that are as pleasing to the eye as they are to the palate. With the highest of standards, we are confident that with your first order, and your first bite, you will become a new and loyal customer.

It has never been easier to select the perfect dessert for your social function. We are a custom cake bakery that specializes in themed custom cakes, cookies, and cupcakes for any occasion. Show us a picture for inspiration, or tell us the theme for your party, and we can come up with a design that fits!
